Describe the project. Include what technologies you used and what you accomplished.

Project Title

Study model, axonometric illustration, and model photos

Study model and finished relief sculpture photos of Colorscapes project. Each color is indicative of a diffferent depth from the base plane further emphasizing the illusion of depth within the piece. Inspired by architectural topography models, the warm palette is reminiscent of a volcanic environment with the most saturated oranges sticking out the furthest and the dark crimson and brown recede into the shadow of their brighter companions.
