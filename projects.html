Colorscapes

Study model, axonometric illustration, and model photos

Study model and finished relief sculpture photos of the Colorscapes project. Each color is indicative of a different depth from the base plane, further emphasizing the illusion of depth within the piece. Inspired by architectural topography models, the warm palette is reminiscent of a volcanic environment with the most saturated oranges sticking out the furthest and the dark crimson and brown receding into the shadow of their brighter companions.
